Our mission is to build accessible conversational agents that help people communicate more naturally with technology.

AI Chatbot Project

Our team is developing an AI Chatbot to explore how conversational agents can enhance communication. We are a small group of researchers designers and engineers focused on building practical features and understanding the human side of interaction.

In the podcast episode we walk through the project's timeline the technical choices we made and the lessons that shaped our current approach. We also highlight the ethics and user privacy considerations we built into the design.

We welcome feedback and collaborators. If you have ideas or would like to contribute please reach out using the contact details on the Meet the Team page.
